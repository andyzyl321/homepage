  • Introducing Guineng Zheng
    I am currently a Ph.D. student in the School of Computing at University of Utah from August 2015. My advisor is Prof. Feifei Li. I am a member of the Data Group. I got my Bachelor's degree and Master's degree both from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. Here is my CV cv.pdf

    From May. 2016 to Aug. 2016 I worked as summer research intern at the security group of NEC Laboratories where I designed and implemented the real-time online stream platform for security audit.

    From Nov. 2014 to Jul. 2015 I worked as a full-time engineer at the big data department of China Merchant Bank where I lead the technical team of risk control group.

    From Apr. 2012 to Oct. 2014 I worked as a full-time engineer at the big data group of Alibaba Cloud Computing where I contributed to the ODPS system which is Alibaba's version of Hadoop and Hive.

    My current research interest is Knowledge Base Population, in particular, we are going to adopt machine learning tecnique to extract knowledge from large-scale corpus.
